SCAM NOTICE

We at N2N are committed to providing a secure and trustworthy experience for all our valued customers and stakeholders. It has come to our attention that certain individuals or groups are engaging in fraudulent activities by misusing the name of our company, affiliated companies or brands for personal gain. We take these matters seriously and are dedicated to taking appropriate action to protect our customers and the integrity of our brand.

Please be aware of the following important information:

  • Unauthorized Websites and Services: We have discovered unauthorized websites or services claiming to be associated with N2N or N2N-AFE. These websites may have similar domain names or use our company's logo and branding to deceive unsuspecting individuals. Please note that we have no affiliation with these unauthorized websites, and any transactions or interactions with them are done at your own risk. Below are a list of known scam websites/links.
    • https://n2n-down.ibhassea.com/
    • https://w.afe-n2n.com/index/login/login/token/b197b56e9fe2e9a7ef538b235b026665.html
  • Phishing Emails and Communication: Scammers may send fraudulent emails, messages, or make phone calls impersonating representatives of N2N or N2N-AFE. These communications often request personal information, financial details, or attempt to redirect you to fake websites for malicious purposes. Be cautious of any unsolicited communications and avoid sharing sensitive information.
  • Payment Requests and Fraudulent Transactions: Scammers may request payment for services, products, or fees using our company's name. These requests are fraudulent, and we never ask for payment outside of our official channels. Please ensure that you only make payments through our verified payment platforms and channels to prevent financial losses.

To protect yourself from scams and fraudulent activities, please take note of the following precautions:

  • Verify the authenticity of any communication, website, or service associated with N2N or N2N-AFE. Use our official channels, contact customer support, or visit our verified website for confirmation.
  • Exercise caution with unsolicited communications, especially those asking for personal or financial information or immediate payment. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  • Regularly review your financial statements and transactions to spot any unauthorized activity. Report any discrepancies immediately to your financial institution and notify our customer support.
  • Stay informed about the latest scams and fraudulent tactics by regularly visiting our Scam Notice page.
